# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Operational realism | Accounts for rollout, rollback, dependencies, and failure modes. | Assumes ideal conditions. |
| System mapping | Explains how components, environments, or workflows connect. | Skips the relationships between moving parts. |
| Readiness | Leaves operators with a concrete readiness or handoff artifact. | Stops before the artifact is usable. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
