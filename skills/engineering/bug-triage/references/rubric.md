# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Evidence hygiene | Separates observed symptoms, user impact, and speculative cause cleanly. | Blends report content and engineer guesswork into one triage narrative. |
| Urgency judgment | Assigns urgency based on actual impact, blast radius, and containment needs. | Uses severity labels as shorthand without explaining the decision logic. |
| Next-step clarity | Leaves the team knowing who should look next, what to verify, and whether mitigation is needed immediately. | Creates a tidy ticket that still leaves the next move ambiguous. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
