# Incident Retrospective Writer

Draft a structured retrospective after an incident with chronology, causes, and follow-ups.

## Quick View

- Category: `engineering`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Post-incident write-ups often lose important causal detail or fail to separate facts from speculation.

## When To Reach For It

- An incident occurred and the follow-up needs a structured write-up.
- The team has facts and notes but not a coherent retrospective.

## What You Should Provide

- timeline
- impact summary
- remediation actions
- open questions

## What You Should Get Back

- retrospective draft
- chronology
- root-cause analysis
- follow-up actions

## Repository Notes

- Main instructions live in `SKILL.md`.
- Machine-readable metadata lives in `skill.json`.
- Examples, rubric, edge cases, and tests live under `references/` and `tests/`.

## Quality Position

This Skill is part of the seeded breadth layer. It is intentionally lighter than the developed and flagship tiers.
