# Customer Proof Extractor

Extract credible customer proof points from interviews, calls, or success stories.

## Quick View

- Category: `gtm`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Proof is often hidden in transcripts and case-study materials rather than extracted for reuse.

## When To Reach For It

- The team needs customer proof for messaging or sales enablement.
- Evidence exists in raw materials but not in reusable form.

## What You Should Provide

- calls or interviews
- customer stories
- target use cases

## What You Should Get Back

- proof point set
- quote candidates
- usage notes
