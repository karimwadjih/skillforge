# Customer Proof Extractor

## Summary

Extract credible customer proof points from interviews, calls, or success stories.

## When To Use

- The team needs customer proof for messaging or sales enablement.
- You need a proof library with evidence statements, quote candidates, and relevance by audience instead of unstructured notes.
- The workflow repeats often enough to justify a reusable asset.

## Avoid When

- There is no defined audience, channel, or launch moment yet.
- You need final legal review or brand approval rather than a working draft.

## Inputs

- calls or interviews
- customer stories
- target use cases

## Output Contract

- proof point set
- quote candidates
- usage notes

## Workflow

1. Confirm the decision context, audience, and missing inputs.
2. Build a proof library with evidence statements, quote candidates, and relevance by audience using the available evidence and constraints.
3. Separate facts from assumptions and make confidence explicit.
4. Finish with next actions, open questions, or escalation notes.

## Deliverable Shape

A proof library with evidence statements, quote candidates, and relevance by audience

## Guardrails

- Do not invent facts that are not present in the supplied materials.
- Make uncertainty explicit when inputs are incomplete or contradictory.
- Keep the output structured enough that another operator can reuse it.
