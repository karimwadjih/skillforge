# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Audience fit | Tailors the output to the buyer, user, or channel context. | Uses generic messaging for every audience. |
| Differentiation | Shows what makes the message or plan distinct and credible. | Reads like interchangeable marketing filler. |
| Operational usefulness | Produces assets a GTM team could use in a real launch or pipeline review. | Creates copy without a usable rollout structure. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
