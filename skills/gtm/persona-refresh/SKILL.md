# Persona Refresh

## Summary

Refresh a persona artifact using newer research, pipeline signals, or product context.

## When To Use

- Existing personas are out of date.
- You need a refreshed persona with role context, goals, blockers, buying triggers, and change notes instead of unstructured notes.
- The workflow repeats often enough to justify a reusable asset.

## Avoid When

- There is no defined audience, channel, or launch moment yet.
- You need final legal review or brand approval rather than a working draft.

## Inputs

- current persona
- new evidence
- strategy context

## Output Contract

- refreshed persona
- changes summary
- open questions

## Workflow

1. Confirm the decision context, audience, and missing inputs.
2. Build a refreshed persona with role context, goals, blockers, buying triggers, and change notes using the available evidence and constraints.
3. Separate facts from assumptions and make confidence explicit.
4. Finish with next actions, open questions, or escalation notes.

## Deliverable Shape

A refreshed persona with role context, goals, blockers, buying triggers, and change notes

## Guardrails

- Do not invent facts that are not present in the supplied materials.
- Make uncertainty explicit when inputs are incomplete or contradictory.
- Keep the output structured enough that another operator can reuse it.
