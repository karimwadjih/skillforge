# Pipeline Review Prep

Prepare a clean pipeline review with deal movement, risks, and leadership discussion points.

## Quick View

- Category: `gtm`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Pipeline meetings often devolve into status reading instead of decision-making.

## When To Reach For It

- A pipeline review meeting needs a better prep artifact.
- Leaders need a concise view of movement, risk, and next decisions.

## What You Should Provide

- pipeline snapshot
- deal notes
- targets
- known risks

## What You Should Get Back

- pipeline review brief
- movement summary
- risk callouts
- decision prompts

- Examples, rubric, edge cases, and tests live under `references/` and `tests/`.
