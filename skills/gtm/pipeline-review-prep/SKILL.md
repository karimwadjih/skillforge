# Pipeline Review Prep

## Summary

Prepare a clean pipeline review with deal movement, risks, and leadership discussion points.

## When To Use

- A pipeline review meeting needs a better prep artifact.
- You need a pipeline review brief with movement, blockers, confidence notes, and decisions needed instead of unstructured notes.
- The workflow repeats often enough to justify a reusable asset.

## Avoid When

- There is no defined audience, channel, or launch moment yet.
- You need final legal review or brand approval rather than a working draft.

## Inputs

- pipeline snapshot
- deal notes
- targets
- known risks

## Output Contract

- pipeline review brief
- movement summary
- risk callouts
- decision prompts

## Workflow

1. Confirm the decision context, audience, and missing inputs.
2. Build a pipeline review brief with movement, blockers, confidence notes, and decisions needed using the available evidence and constraints.
3. Separate facts from assumptions and make confidence explicit.
4. Finish with next actions, open questions, or escalation notes.

## Deliverable Shape

A pipeline review brief with movement, blockers, confidence notes, and decisions needed

## Guardrails

- Do not invent facts that are not present in the supplied materials.
- Make uncertainty explicit when inputs are incomplete or contradictory.
- Keep the output structured enough that another operator can reuse it.

## Maturity Note

This Skill is part of the seeded breadth layer. It is intentionally lighter than the developed and flagship tiers.
