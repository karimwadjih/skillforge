# Launch Plan Generator

Build a practical launch plan with sequencing, owners, messaging dependencies, and readiness checks.

## Quick View

- Category: `gtm`
- Maturity: `frontier`
- Tier: `flagship`
- Evaluation status: `benchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Launches often fail because responsibilities and dependencies remain implicit until late in the process.

## Why This Skill Matters

Launches fail when teams confuse a task list with a launch plan. This skill matters when sequencing, readiness gates, owner handoffs, and post-launch monitoring actually need to line up across functions.

## What Makes It Different

- Builds around launch objective, dependencies, and gating decisions rather than a flat checklist.
- Treats cross-functional handoffs as part of the plan, not a side note.
- Ends with post-launch checks and contingency posture, not just launch-day tasks.

## When To Reach For It

- A launch needs a coordinated GTM plan across functions.
- A team needs a launch artifact with sequencing, dependencies, and readiness logic.

## What You Should Provide

- launch objective
- audience
- timeline
- channels
- constraints

## What You Should Get Back

- launch plan
- owner map
- dependency list
- readiness checkpoints
