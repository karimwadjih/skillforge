# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Account specificity | Feels tailored to the actual account, stage, and stakeholder mix rather than reusable sales wallpaper. | Could describe almost any customer call. |
| Objection anticipation | Names the likely objections and the proof or posture each one requires. | Mentions risk vaguely without preparing the team for the actual pushback. |
| Conversation leverage | Improves what the team should do in the meeting and immediately after it. | Summarizes context without changing the conversation strategy. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
