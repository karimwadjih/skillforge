# Happy Path Tests

## Renewal rescue call happy path

### Prompt

Prepare a renewal rescue call brief that sharpens the customer context, likely objections, and the strongest conversation path for the account team.

### Checks

- Frame the real deal risk instead of just summarizing the account record.
- Anticipate executive and operator objections separately.
- Recommend the next-best conversation move rather than a generic pitch sequence.

## Expansion call with skeptical operator happy path

### Prompt

Build a call brief for the expansion meeting that accounts for operator skepticism, executive upside, and the most credible proof sequence.

### Checks

- Treat implementation burden as a central objection, not a side note.
- Balance executive value framing with operator-level credibility.
- Recommend a conversation move that earns the next step instead of overselling the expansion.
