# Sales Call Brief

Prepare an account-specific call brief with stakeholder intent, likely objections, and the best next move.

## Quick View

- Category: `gtm`
- Maturity: `frontier`
- Tier: `developed`
- Evaluation status: `sampled`

## What This Skill Does

Teams often overprepare on product facts and underprepare on stakeholder dynamics, proof quality, and the one move most likely to change the conversation.

## When To Reach For It

- A high-stakes prospect, renewal, or escalation call needs sharper prep than the CRM notes provide.
- The account history is noisy enough that the team needs one grounded pre-call artifact before walking in.

## What You Should Provide

- account context and stage
- recent activity or call notes
- meeting objective
- known stakeholders and risks
- available proof points or weak spots

## What You Should Get Back

- sales call brief
- stakeholder-specific talk track priorities
- objection prep
- follow-up plan
