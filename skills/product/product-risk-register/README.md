# Product Risk Register

Create a product risk register for an initiative before the team commits fully.

## Quick View

- Category: `product`
- Maturity: `scaffold`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Product risks are often discussed informally and then forgotten until they materialize.

## When To Reach For It

- A product initiative is moving forward without a clear risk register.
- Leaders want a structured view of product risks before approval.

## What You Should Provide

- initiative scope
- timeline
- dependencies
- known concerns

## What You Should Get Back

- risk register
- severity view
- mitigation notes

## Repository Notes

- Main instructions live in `SKILL.md`.
- Machine-readable metadata lives in `skill.json`.
- Examples, rubric, edge cases, and tests live under `references/` and `tests/`.

## Quality Position

This Skill is part of the seeded breadth layer. It is intentionally lighter than the developed and flagship tiers.
