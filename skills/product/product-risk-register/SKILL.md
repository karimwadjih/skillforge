# Product Risk Register

## Summary

Create a product risk register for an initiative before the team commits fully.

## When To Use

- A product initiative is moving forward without a clear risk register.
- You need a product risk register with severity, probability, mitigation, owners, and review cadence instead of unstructured notes.
- The workflow repeats often enough to justify a reusable asset.

## Avoid When

- The team has not defined the product area or user segment at all yet.
- You need direct user validation rather than a synthesis artifact.

## Inputs

- initiative scope
- timeline
- dependencies
- known concerns

## Output Contract

- risk register
- severity view
- mitigation notes

## Workflow

1. Confirm the decision context, audience, and missing inputs.
2. Build a product risk register with severity, probability, mitigation, owners, and review cadence using the available evidence and constraints.
3. Separate facts from assumptions and make confidence explicit.
4. Finish with next actions, open questions, or escalation notes.

## Deliverable Shape

A product risk register with severity, probability, mitigation, owners, and review cadence

## Guardrails

- Do not invent facts that are not present in the supplied materials.
- Make uncertainty explicit when inputs are incomplete or contradictory.
- Keep the output structured enough that another operator can reuse it.

## Maturity Note

This Skill is part of the seeded breadth layer. It is intentionally lighter than the developed and flagship tiers.
