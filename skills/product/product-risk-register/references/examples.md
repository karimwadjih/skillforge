# Examples

## Existing workflow under pressure

### Scenario

A team needs product risk register support for a real deadline instead of a brainstorming session.

### Prompt

Use Product Risk Register to produce a product risk register with severity, probability, mitigation, owners, and review cadence for the current workstream.

### Strong Response Should

- State the workflow objective in plain language.
- Produce a product risk register with severity, probability, mitigation, owners, and review cadence with explicit structure.
- Call out missing information and the next decision.
