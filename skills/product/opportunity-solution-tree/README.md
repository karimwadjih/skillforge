# Opportunity Solution Tree

Structure an opportunity solution tree from product goals, opportunities, and candidate solutions.

## Quick View

- Category: `product`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Discovery work often jumps between goals and solutions without a visible map of the reasoning.

## When To Reach For It

- The team wants to connect a product goal to candidate discovery paths.
- Discovery conversations need more structure than a backlog list.

## What You Should Provide

- goal
- known opportunities
- candidate ideas
- constraints

## What You Should Get Back

- opportunity tree
- assumption notes
- discovery next steps

## Repository Notes

- Main instructions live in `SKILL.md`.
- Machine-readable metadata lives in `skill.json`.
- Examples, rubric, edge cases, and tests live under `references/` and `tests/`.
