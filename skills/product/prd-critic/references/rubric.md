# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Problem truthfulness | Tests whether the document describes a real user or business problem with enough evidence to justify action. | Accepts abstract pain points and solution-first framing at face value. |
| Scope discipline | Exposes where the PRD quietly expands scope, hides dependencies, or leaves decision boundaries loose. | Mentions 'scope risk' generically without pointing to the specific ambiguity. |
| Commitment readiness | Leaves the team knowing whether to proceed, revise, or stop and gather more evidence. | Produces a critique that sounds smart but does not change the decision path. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
