# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Problem framing | Clarifies the user problem, evidence strength, and scope boundary. | Confuses solutions with the actual problem. |
| Tradeoff rigor | Surfaces tradeoffs, risks, and assumptions explicitly. | Presents one direction as obvious without evaluating alternatives. |
| Execution readiness | Produces an output another product team member could act on. | Leaves next steps vague or underspecified. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
