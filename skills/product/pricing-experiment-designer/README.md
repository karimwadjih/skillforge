# Pricing Experiment Designer

Design a pricing experiment with hypotheses, guardrails, and decision thresholds.

## Quick View

- Category: `product`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Pricing experiments are easy to suggest and easy to run badly without clear decision thresholds or guardrails.

## When To Reach For It

- A team wants to test pricing without creating avoidable operational risk.
- Pricing discussion needs a structured experiment design.

## What You Should Provide

- pricing hypothesis
- customer segment
- constraints
- guardrails

## What You Should Get Back

- experiment plan
- success thresholds
- risk notes

## Repository Notes

- Main instructions live in `SKILL.md`.
- Machine-readable metadata lives in `skill.json`.
- Examples, rubric, edge cases, and tests live under `references/` and `tests/`.

## Quality Position

This Skill is part of the seeded breadth layer. It is intentionally lighter than the developed and flagship tiers.
