# Churn Insight Analyzer

Analyze churn signals and convert them into actionable themes and retention hypotheses.

## Quick View

- Category: `product`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Teams track churn numbers but struggle to turn them into coherent product decisions.

## When To Reach For It

- The team needs a better story behind churn than top-level metrics alone.
- Retention work needs hypothesis generation from mixed evidence.

## What You Should Provide

- churn metrics
- customer feedback
- segment context

## What You Should Get Back

- churn theme summary
- retention hypotheses
- follow-up questions
