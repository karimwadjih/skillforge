# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Signal discrimination | Separates recurring pain, emerging signal, and anecdotal noise with explicit confidence. | Reports every interview insight at the same level of importance. |
| Segment integrity | Shows where different user groups disagree or care about different parts of the workflow. | Blends all interviewees into one generic customer voice. |
| Decision translation | Connects interview evidence to concrete product, research, or GTM moves. | Stops at descriptive themes and leaves the team to infer the implications. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
