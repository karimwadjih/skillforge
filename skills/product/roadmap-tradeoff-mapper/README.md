# Roadmap Tradeoff Mapper

Compare competing roadmap bets with explicit upside, delay cost, dependency drag, and a forced sequence.

## Quick View

- Category: `product`
- Maturity: `frontier`
- Tier: `developed`
- Evaluation status: `sampled`

## What This Skill Does

Roadmap reviews often sound rigorous while hiding the real question: what gets delayed, which dependency becomes gating, and which option still lacks evidence to deserve scarce capacity.

## When To Reach For It

- Two or more roadmap bets are competing for the same quarter and every argument sounds plausible in isolation.
- Stakeholders keep ranking ideas without showing what each choice delays, blocks, or makes harder later.

## What You Should Provide

- candidate roadmap options
- business or product goals
- constraints and capacity assumptions
- dependencies and timing risks
- known evidence for each option

## What You Should Get Back

- tradeoff map
- option-by-option comparison
- recommended sequence or cut line
- open evidence gaps
