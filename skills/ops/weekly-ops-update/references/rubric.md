# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Signal density | Pulls forward the few changes, blockers, and shifts that actually matter this week. | Reads like a chronological dump of work completed. |
| Operational interpretation | Explains what the movement means for the operating plan, not just which number moved. | Reports metrics and incidents without interpreting their operational weight. |
| Action linkage | Connects each important risk or change to owners, decisions, or next checks. | Leaves leaders with awareness but no clear follow-up path. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
