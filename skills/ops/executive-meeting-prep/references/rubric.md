# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Decision framing | Clarifies what must be decided, what is still open, and what recommendation is on the table. | Offers context without making the decision moments explicit. |
| Executive compression | Reduces complex material into the smallest set of facts and tradeoffs leaders need. | Forces executives to read through staff-level detail to find the actual issue. |
| Room readiness | Prepares the team for pushback, follow-up asks, and unresolved risks that will surface live. | Ends once the brief is written and leaves the team exposed in the meeting. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
