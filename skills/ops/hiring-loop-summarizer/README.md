# Hiring Loop Summarizer

Summarize a hiring loop with themes, risks, and decision support notes.

## Quick View

- Category: `ops`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Hiring feedback can be lengthy, inconsistent, and difficult to convert into a fair summary.

## When To Reach For It

- A hiring panel needs a better synthesis of interview feedback.
- Interview notes exist but not a decision-ready summary.

## What You Should Provide

- interview feedback
- role rubric
- known concerns

## What You Should Get Back

- loop summary
- signal themes
- decision support notes

## Repository Notes

- Main instructions live in `SKILL.md`.
- Machine-readable metadata lives in `skill.json`.
- Examples, rubric, edge cases, and tests live under `references/` and `tests/`.

## Quality Position

This Skill is part of the seeded breadth layer. It is intentionally lighter than the developed and flagship tiers.
