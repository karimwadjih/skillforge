# Operating Rhythm Designer

Design an operating rhythm with clear decision cadence, artifact discipline, and escalation paths.

## Quick View

- Category: `ops`
- Maturity: `frontier`
- Tier: `developed`
- Evaluation status: `sampled`

## What This Skill Does

Organizations accumulate recurring meetings faster than they build the artifacts, ownership, and escalation logic needed to make those meetings useful.

## When To Reach For It

- The team has too many meetings, too little decision clarity, or too much status noise between leaders.
- An organization is scaling past founder or ad hoc coordination and needs a repeatable operating cadence.

## What You Should Provide

- org shape and key functions
- recurring decisions that need cadence support
- metrics and reporting availability
- existing meetings or rituals
- known escalation failures

## What You Should Get Back

- operating rhythm design
- meeting and artifact cadence map
- owner assignments
- escalation rules
