# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Decision cadence fit | Matches each recurring forum to the type and speed of decisions it should support. | Creates calendar structure without improving decision timing. |
| Artifact discipline | Names the artifact, owner, and expected level of preparation required for each cadence point. | Treats meetings as sufficient without defining the inputs that make them useful. |
| Escalation clarity | Shows what gets escalated, to whom, and on what trigger instead of relying on improvisation. | Mentions escalation but leaves the actual route ambiguous. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
