# Market Signal Scanner

Collect and rank external market signals for a team that needs a fast situational read.

## Quick View

- Category: `research`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Signal reviews often become noisy roundups instead of a prioritized picture of what changed and why it matters.

## When To Reach For It

- A market is changing and the team needs a concise weekly or monthly view.
- You need to separate important external signals from background noise.

## What You Should Provide

- signal list
- industry context
- time window

## What You Should Get Back

- ranked signal summary
- implication notes
- watch list
