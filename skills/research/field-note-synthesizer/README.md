# Field Note Synthesizer

Turn raw field notes into themes, anomalies, and follow-up questions.

## Quick View

- Category: `research`
- Maturity: `experimental`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Raw observational notes degrade quickly when nobody turns them into a structured artifact.

## When To Reach For It

- A team gathered observational notes and needs a reliable synthesis.
- Field notes contain patterns that are easy to miss in raw form.

## What You Should Provide

- field notes
- observation goal
- known constraints

## What You Should Get Back

- theme summary
- anomalies
- follow-up questions
