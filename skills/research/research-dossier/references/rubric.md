# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Evidence traceability | Lets a reader see which claims are grounded, which are inferred, and where the evidence is weak. | Blends source material and interpretation into one authoritative voice. |
| Tension handling | Shows contradictions, edge cases, and open questions without collapsing them into false consensus. | Hides messy signals to keep the brief sounding tidy. |
| Decision force | Leaves the reader with a recommendation, the conditions behind it, and what evidence could change it. | Ends as a polished note dump with no decision posture. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
