# Happy Path Tests

## Market entry briefing happy path 1

### Prompt

Synthesize analyst notes, competitor pricing snapshots, customer calls, and internal assumptions into a market-entry dossier.

### Checks

- Separate facts, inferences, and assumptions.
- Map the strongest signals and the biggest unknowns.
- End with a clear recommendation on whether to proceed, pause, or research further.

## Interview-heavy synthesis happy path 2

### Prompt

Create a dossier from the interview notes, call recordings, and prior product metrics.

### Checks

- Cluster recurring themes without erasing disagreement.
- Show which findings are well supported and which are tentative.
- Translate the findings into product or GTM implications.
