# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Source quality | Uses credible evidence, distinguishes primary and secondary material, and flags weak sourcing. | Treats all sources as equal or cites unsupported assertions. |
| Synthesis depth | Connects evidence into themes, tradeoffs, and decision-relevant insights. | Restates findings without explaining why they matter. |
| Decision usefulness | Ends with a brief that helps a team decide what to do next. | Leaves the reader with a pile of notes and no recommendation. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
