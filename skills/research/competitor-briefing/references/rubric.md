# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Signal discrimination | Separates durable strategic movement from packaging, rumor, and launch-day theater. | Treats every new competitor artifact as equally meaningful. |
| Strategic restraint | Protects the team from mirror-response behavior and recommends action only where the evidence justifies it. | Rewards overreaction and turns every competitor move into internal roadmap turbulence. |
| Internal actionability | Names which product, GTM, or research responses are warranted now, later, or not at all. | Ends as commentary with no clear internal posture. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
