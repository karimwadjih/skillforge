# Rubric

| Criterion | Strong | Weak |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Method transparency | States exactly what the benchmark measures, what it does not, and how cases and rubrics were chosen. | Presents results as authoritative without exposing the design choices underneath. |
| Interpretation discipline | Explains what score changes mean in operational terms and where interpretation should stop. | Recites numbers without connecting them to a real decision. |
| Caveat honesty | Makes limitations impossible to miss and prevents benchmark theater. | Treats caveats as legal fine print after the headline has already overclaimed. |

## Certification Note

Certified status requires benchmark coverage, score threshold, and `manual_review: passed`. It is not granted by structure alone.
