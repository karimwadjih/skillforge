# Policy Clarifier

Clarify a policy or internal guideline into a more readable, operationally useful document.

## Quick View

- Category: `docs`
- Maturity: `scaffold`
- Tier: `seeded`
- Evaluation status: `unbenchmarked`

## What This Skill Does

Policy documents often optimize for completeness while sacrificing usability.

## When To Reach For It

- A policy document needs clearer language and action framing.
- Teams need help translating a policy into day-to-day implications.

## What You Should Provide

- policy text
- audience
- common confusion points

## What You Should Get Back

- clarified policy draft
- operational guidance
- FAQ candidates

## Repository Notes

- Main instructions live in `SKILL.md`.
- Machine-readable metadata lives in `skill.json`.
- Examples, rubric, edge cases, and tests live under `references/` and `tests/`.
