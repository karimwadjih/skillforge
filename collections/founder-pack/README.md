# Founder Pack

A compact operating set for founders who need faster research, tighter product judgment, and cleaner weekly decision artifacts.

## Audience

Founders and startup operators

## Included Skills

- `research-dossier`: Turn scattered research inputs into a decision-ready dossier with evidence, themes, and unresolved questions.
- `prd-critic`: Critique a product requirements document for clarity, scope, risk, and execution readiness.
- `launch-plan-generator`: Build a practical launch plan with sequencing, owners, messaging dependencies, and readiness checks.
- `weekly-ops-update`: Build a weekly operating update that highlights signal, risk, and follow-ups without reading like status spam.
- `executive-meeting-prep`: Prepare a leadership meeting brief with objectives, decisions, risks, and likely questions.
- `sales-call-brief`: Prepare an account-specific call brief with stakeholder intent, likely objections, and the best next move.

## Intended Use Cases

- Pressure-test strategy and roadmap calls
- Prepare sharper launch and revenue conversations
- Run a weekly operating cadence without status bloat

## Quick Start

1. Pick one operating problem that repeats every week.
2. Use the most relevant flagship skill first, then expand into related pack assets.
