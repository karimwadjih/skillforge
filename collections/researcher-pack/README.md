# Researcher Pack

A synthesis-heavy pack for teams turning scattered evidence into dossiers, literature maps, and benchmark framing.

## Audience

Researchers, strategy teams, and analyst functions

## Included Skills

- `research-dossier`: Turn scattered research inputs into a decision-ready dossier with evidence, themes, and unresolved questions.
- `benchmark-brief`: Create a concise benchmark brief that explains what is being measured, how it is scored, and how to interpret the results.
- `literature-map`: Map a crowded topic into schools of thought, evidence weight, and the shortest smart reading path.
- `competitor-briefing`: Turn competitor noise into a briefing about intent, durability, and what your team should ignore.
- `source-credibility-audit`: Audit a set of sources for credibility, bias, recency, and usefulness.
- `field-note-synthesizer`: Turn raw field notes into themes, anomalies, and follow-up questions.

## Intended Use Cases

- Orient quickly in a new domain
- Convert evidence into decision-ready briefs
- Separate credibility review from benchmark design

## Quick Start

1. Use Research Dossier for broad synthesis and Literature Map for early orientation.
2. Keep credibility and benchmark framing separate instead of mixing them into one document.
