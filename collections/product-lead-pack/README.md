# Product Lead Pack

A working set for product leads moving from raw inputs to tighter specs, cleaner tradeoffs, and better decisions.

## Audience

Product leads and principal product managers

## Included Skills

- `prd-critic`: Critique a product requirements document for clarity, scope, risk, and execution readiness.
- `customer-interview-synthesizer`: Turn multiple customer interviews into a synthesis with themes, tensions, and product implications.
- `roadmap-tradeoff-mapper`: Compare competing roadmap bets with explicit upside, delay cost, dependency drag, and a forced sequence.
- `feature-spec-refiner`: Tighten a draft spec until scope, state changes, acceptance criteria, and handoff risk are explicit.
- `benchmark-brief`: Create a concise benchmark brief that explains what is being measured, how it is scored, and how to interpret the results.
- `research-dossier`: Turn scattered research inputs into a decision-ready dossier with evidence, themes, and unresolved questions.

## Intended Use Cases

- Turn research into roadmap choices
- Sharpen PRDs and implementation handoffs
- Compare competing bets before quarter commitments

## Quick Start

1. Start with the skill closest to the current artifact you already have.
2. Use the output as input to the next product planning step.
