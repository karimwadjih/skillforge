# Certification Review Checklist

Use this checklist before changing any Skill to `certified`.

## Required evidence

- The Skill has a clear repeated workflow and a precise trigger.
- `SKILL.md` and `skill.json` are consistent.
- The rubric is specific enough to evaluate real outputs.
- The Skill includes at least three realistic examples.
- The Skill includes at least three meaningful tests across happy, edge, and failure paths.
- Benchmark assets exist in `evals/`.
- The latest score is at least 90.
- `manual_review` is set to `passed`.

## Manual review expectations

- Instructions are distinct rather than template-like.
- The output structure is concrete and useful.
- Edge cases are operationally credible.
- Failure modes are acknowledged instead of hidden.
- Public copy does not overclaim maturity or compatibility.
