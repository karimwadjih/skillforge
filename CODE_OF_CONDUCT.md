# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in the Skillforge community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, experience level, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Demonstrating empathy and respect
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback
- Focusing on what improves the project
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experience levels

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, intimidation, or discrimination
- Trolling or insulting language
- Publishing private information without consent
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and may take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and external representations of the project.

## Reporting

Report concerns to the maintainers through repository contacts listed in project metadata or issue templates.
